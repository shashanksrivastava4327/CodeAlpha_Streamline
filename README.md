## 📖 Project Overview

**Streamline** is a Netflix Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project developed using Python. The objective is to identify patterns, trends, and valuable insights regarding Netflix movies and TV shows.

The project includes data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, and interpretation of results through multiple charts and graphs.


The project includes data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, and interpretation of results through multiple charts and graphs.

---

🎯 Objectives

✅ Understand Netflix content distribution

✅ Compare Movies and TV Shows

✅ Analyze content growth over the years

✅ Identify top contributing countries

✅ Explore ratings distribution

✅ Discover popular genres and directors

✅ Detect outliers in release years

🔍 Key Findings

📌 Movies Dominate Netflix

Netflix contains significantly more Movies than TV Shows.

📌 USA Leads Content Production

The United States contributes the highest amount of Netflix content.

📌 Rapid Growth After 2015

A sharp increase in content addition is observed after 2015.

📌 Popular Genres Identified

International Movies and Dramas are among the most common genres.

📌 Rating Patterns Observed

TV-MA and TV-14 ratings dominate the platform.
